Programs & Initiatives

Five thematic areas driving sustainable change across Tamil Nadu - from grassroots research and livelihood development to skill training, capacity building, and evidence-based planning.

Community Outreach & Awareness

Community Outreach & Awareness

C

Grassroots awareness campaigns and community-centered interventions focused on health, sanitation, education, tribal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion. These initiatives strengthen public participation while addressing immediate local needs through one-day programs, workshops, and village-level engagement activities.

Drug Awareness Programs WASH & Hygiene Campaigns COVID Relief & Welfare Independence Day & Events Educational Material Support Tribal Welfare Programs Child Protection Awareness Public Health Awareness

Programs Conducted

Drug awareness campaigns in rural communities
COVID-19 relief distribution drives in Thanjavur, Yercaud, and Thiruvallur
WASH awareness initiatives with community participation
Educational scholarship support for women students in Ariyalur
Tribal welfare school book donation programs
Herbal sanitary napkin awareness in Chennai
Community celebration and welfare outreach programs
02
Livelihoods & Rural Dev

Livelihood Promotion & Rural Development

L

Supporting sustainable livelihoods through agriculture, millet promotion, artisan development, renewable energy awareness, and rural entrepreneurship activities. These interventions focus on improving income opportunities while preserving traditional knowledge and eco-friendly practices.

Millet Promotion Programs Organic Farming Awareness Value-Added Food Training Rural Entrepreneurship Tribal Handicraft Promotion Renewable Energy Awareness Solar Livelihood Initiatives Artisan Development

Programs Conducted

Millet cultivation and processing workshops in Chengalpattu and rural clusters
Value-added food production training programs
Solar lantern distribution and renewable energy awareness drives
Tribal handicraft awareness and artisan engagement activities
Rural entrepreneurship and micro-enterprise support programs
Community-based agriculture and sustainability awareness initiatives
03
Skill Development

Skill Development & Employability

S

Short-term and community-based skill initiatives aimed at improving employability, entrepreneurship, and self-reliance among youth and women. Programs combine vocational exposure, technical orientation, and livelihood-focused training across rural and semi-urban regions.

Tailoring & Embroidery Handicraft Skill Exposure Healthcare Orientation IT & Digital Literacy Entrepreneurship Awareness Retail & Employability Skills Soft Skills & Career Guidance Youth Capacity Building

Programs Conducted

PMKVY-linked community skill training activities
Hand embroidery and handicraft exposure programs with NIFT collaboration
Robotics and innovation awareness sessions at educational institutions
Youth employability and entrepreneurship workshops
Tailoring, mehendi, and beautician livelihood training
Career guidance and workplace readiness sessions for rural youth
04
Women Empowerment

Women & Community Empowerment

W

Women-centered initiatives that strengthen self-help groups, improve financial awareness, encourage leadership participation, and promote home-based livelihood opportunities for marginalized women and vulnerable communities.

SHG Strengthening Financial Literacy Home-Based Livelihoods Women Entrepreneurship Leadership Development Community Participation Livelihood Awareness Programs Welfare Support Activities

Programs Conducted

Agarbatti packing and home-based livelihood support activities
SHG capacity-building and enterprise awareness sessions
Women entrepreneurship and income-generation workshops
Community participation programs for women and vulnerable groups
Livelihood support initiatives for widowed and elderly women

Research, Capacity Building & Participatory Planning

Participatory assessments, grassroots consultations, institutional engagement, and evidence-based planning initiatives that strengthen local governance, improve development strategies, and encourage community-led decision-making.

Participatory Rural Appraisals Community Consultations Baseline Assessments Village-Level Planning Institutional Capacity Building Stakeholder Workshops Monitoring & Documentation Knowledge Sharing Platforms

Programs Conducted

Institutional coordination and participatory planning sessions
Capacity-building activities for local groups and volunteers
Monitoring, documentation, and impact-learning initiatives
